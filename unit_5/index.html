All Natural Botanical Body Care

Sauvage Fleur is an all natural body care company based in Ventura, California. Our products are handmade in small batches. They are safe, gentle, and very effective! Most of our line is vegan and none of our products are tested on animals. We don't use synthetic fragrances, binders, thickeners, emulsifiers, preservatives, petroleum based oils, or any other toxic chemicals.

We are a unique company that specializes in using various flower extracts for the promotion and maintenance of youthful, natural beauty. We also offer an intoxicating line of olde world style colognes that are made with pure flower oils, plus much more.

Sauvage Fleur Practices

  • We specialize in using flower extracts to promote youthful, natural beauty.
  • We are committed to sourcing out ingredients that are pure and grown without agrochemicals.
  • Our products are all botanical and we do not use synthetic fragrances or other petrochemical ingredients.
  • Our products and ingredients are not tested on animals and most of our line is suitable for vegans. (The one non-vegan ingredient that is sometimes used is locally sourced beeswax.)
